National Institute of Homoeopathy (NIH) is an autonomous organisation under the Department of Ayurveda, Yoga & Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha and Homoeopathy (AYUSH), Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Govt. of India.

It is affiliated to the West Bengal University of Health Sciences and conducts the degree course in Homoeopathy ie Bachelor of Homoeopathic Medicine and Surgery [B.H.M.S.] since 1987 and Post - Graduate course i.e. Doctor of Medicine in Homoeopathy (M.D.(Hom.) in three subjects viz Organon of Medicine, Repertory and Materia Medica since 1998-99.

The Institute has a 250-bed hospital, with 10 for surgery and 10 for maternity cases. National Institute of Homoeopathy was established on 10 December 1975 in Kolkata as an Autonomous Organisation under the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Govt. of India. The then Institute building was located at 118 Amherst Street, Kolkata 700 009. Later it was shifted to the present location.
