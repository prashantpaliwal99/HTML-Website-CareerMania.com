Unani System of medicine, whose history is traced back to the 2nd century, has been rending service to the mankind for thousands of years and is not only surviving but also solving the health problems faced by the suffering humanity.The history and origin of Unani medicine is traced back to Claudius Galenus of Pergamum, who lived in the 2nd century of the Christian era and also to ancient Iranian Medicine, the basic knowledge of Unani Medicine as a healing system was developed by Hakim Ibn Sina (known Avicinna in the west) in his medical encyclopedia, “The Canon of Medicine”. While he was primarily influenced by Greek and Islamic medicine, he was also influenced by Indian Medical Teaching of Sushrutha and Charaka.

The Unani system of Medicine utilizes a holistic set of principles which help a practitioner to turn a diagnosis into a treatment process. The philosophy behind the approach is said to be based on the teachings of Hippocrates, Avicenna and Galen.

SCOPE AND FUTURE OF UNANI SYSTEM OF BUMS (UNANI MEDICINE) COURSE:

Due to the limitations of modern medicine and the general realization that anything natural is safer and more in accordance with health, herbs have been used in medicine since the beginning of time and thus there is a great demand for the alternate systems of medicine. Tremendous potential for traditional medicines due to long history of the ‘Doctrine of Certainty’ people are now rediscovering their strong ethnic roots and reinvestigating old values.

With the recognition of Unani etc systems of medicine by the developed countries like America, as Complementary or Alternate Medicine (CAM) is gaining more demand and popularity of the alternate systems of medicine. Unani System of Medicine has a great potential towards maintaining good health. Today more and more people are turning towards this holistic approach towards a healthy living through Unani System of Medicine.

Unani is gaining acceptance among the people and so the BUMS course has good employment and career prospects. BUMS degree makes one eligible to seek employment in government health care units, dispensaries, voluntary organizations, medical colleges and corporate hospitals. One can also work in Unani Medical Colleges in the academic field. One can pursue further study and can also explore options in clinical research.

For those who want to continue their education after the completion of BUMS (Bachelor of Unani Medicine and Surgery) can take up MD (Doctor of Medicine) in Unani medicine, which is a post graduate degree conferred by many institutions.

DURATION OF BUMS (UNANI MEDICINE) COURSE:

The duration of BUMS course is five and half years which includes four and half years of training and one year of compulsory rotatory internship.

ELIGIBILITY FOR BUMS COURSE:

Eligibility for BUMS course is 10+2 with a science stream with physics,chemistry and biology, and a minimum of 45% marks in the qualifying examination. The candidate must have completed a minimum of 17 years of age.
