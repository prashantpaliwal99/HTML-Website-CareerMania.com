MBBS Entrance is one of the most competitive entrance in India and here is some useful information for students who aspire to do the MBBS course in India. Bachelor in Medicine and Bachelor in Surgery MBBS is a 4 and half year course and is followed by 1 year compulsory internship.

The course is structured as follows MBBS Pre Clinical Semester 1 and 2

Includes basic science courses in year one like Anatomy, Bio chemistry, Physiology etc.

MBBS Para Clinical Semester 3-5

Includes subjects like Community Medicine; Forensic Medicine, Pathology, Pharmacology, Microbiology, pharmacology, forensic medicine, ophthalmology, NT, medicine, surgery, gynecology, pediatrics etc. , Clinical postings in wards, OPDs to begin here

MBBS Clinical Semester 6-9

Community Medicine, Medicine and allied subjects (Psychiatry, Dermatology); Obst. Gynae.; Pediatrics;Surgery and allied subjects (Anesthesiology,E.N.T., Ophthalmology, Orthopedics); Clinical postings

Admission to MBBS Course in India

There are many entrances that students can take to get admission in MBBS course. One of the main National enrance exam is the All India Medical entrance conducted by CBSE called AIPMT 2015. This All India Medical Entrance test is conducted for 15% seats of MBBS course in Medical/ Dental Colleges of India and for 100% seats of MBBS course in some participating Universities and states. The counseling for successful candidates under 15% quota will be conducted by DGHS and participating states/ universities and Institutions. Other than this AIIMS which is the top ranked institute in India, conducts its own national entrance exam.
